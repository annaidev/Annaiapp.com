Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Annai collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use Annaiapp.com, the Annai Travel Planner website, and related mobile applications and services (together, the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: username, password, security question, and security answer for account creation and recovery.
  • Profile information: preferred language, home currency, citizenship, and other travel preferences you choose to save.
  • Trip content: starting location, destination, trip type, dates, notes, itinerary items, packing lists, budget items, travel documents, and booking-import content you submit.
  • AI requests and outputs: prompts, questions, trip context, and generated responses for AI-powered features.
  • Subscription and purchase data: product IDs, subscription status, expiry dates, sandbox status, and store account identifiers used to validate Apple App Store and Google Play purchases. Annai does not collect your full payment card details.
  • Technical and session data: cookies, session identifiers, IP address, request logs, browser/device details, and similar operational data needed to run and secure the Service.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service.
  • Authenticate users and keep accounts secure.
  • Save, sync, and display your trips and travel content.
  • Generate AI-powered travel outputs you request.
  • Validate subscriptions, restore purchases, and manage access.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Respond to support issues, legal requests, and safety concerns.
  • Improve product performance, reliability, and user experience.

3. AI and Third-Party Processing

Some features send the content you provide, including trip context and questions, to third-party AI providers such as OpenAI in order to generate responses. AI features are optional and may be limited to paid plans. You should avoid submitting highly sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for the feature you are using.

4. Service Providers and Sharing

Annai may share information with service providers that help operate the Service, such as cloud hosting, database, authentication, customer support, AI, and mobile subscription-validation providers. Annai may also receive subscription and purchase-status information from Apple and Google to verify entitlements.

Annai may also share information when required by law, to enforce our terms, to protect users or the public, or in connection with a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

5. Third-Party Links and Travel Providers

The Service may contain links to airlines, hotels, booking platforms, maps, government sites, and other third-party services. If you click or use a third-party service, that third party’s own privacy practices apply. Annai is not responsible for the privacy or content practices of third-party sites or apps.

6. Cookies and Sessions

Annai uses cookies and session technologies to keep you signed in, secure the Service, and support core account functions. If you block necessary cookies, some parts of the Service may not work properly.

7. Data Retention

Annai keeps personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Service, maintain account records, validate subscriptions, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the purpose for which it was collected.

8. Security

Annai uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and Annai cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13, and Annai does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will review the request.

10. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of certain personal information. You may also have rights to object to certain processing or request a copy of your information. To make a request, contact us at the email below.

You may also delete your Annai account in-app through the Account page, use the public deletion page at /account-deletion/, or request deletion of your account and associated personal information by contacting annaiapp@proton.me.

11. International Use

Annai may process and store information in countries other than your own. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws than those in your location.

12. Changes to This Policy

Annai may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use of the Service after an update means the updated policy will apply going forward.

13. Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to annaiapp@proton.me.

This is a practical first-pass privacy policy for Annai’s current product and data flows. It is not legal advice and should be reviewed by a qualified attorney before broad commercial rollout.
