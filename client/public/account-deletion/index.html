Account Deletion

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Annai lets users delete their account directly inside the app. This page exists as the public account-deletion reference for users who cannot currently access the app.

Delete Your Account In-App

  1. Sign in to Annai Travel Planner.
  2. Open the Account page.
  3. Go to the Delete Account section.
  4. Type DELETE and confirm the deletion prompt.

What Gets Deleted

  • your Annai account
  • trip records
  • itinerary items
  • packing lists
  • budget items
  • travel documents
  • active session access

If You Cannot Access the App

Email annaiapp@proton.me from the address or account context associated with your request and include your Annai username if available.

Important Subscription Note

Deleting your Annai account does not automatically cancel an Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. Manage or cancel the subscription through the store where you purchased it.
