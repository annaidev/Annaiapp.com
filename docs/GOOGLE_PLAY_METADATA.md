## Full Description

Annai Travel Planner helps you organize trips from one place.

Plan your itinerary, track your travel budget, manage packing lists, store important travel documents, and keep key destination details close at hand. Annai Pro adds AI-powered trip help, destination insights, safety guidance, local phrases, weather help, and premium planning tools.

With Annai Travel Planner you can:
- create and manage trips
- build itinerary plans day by day
- track budgets and expenses
- organize travel documents and confirmations
- manage packing lists
- review destination and customs-entry guidance

Annai Pro unlocks:
- Ask Annai trip assistant
- AI trip plan generation
- AI safety and embassy guidance
- AI local phrases and weather support
- premium map and location tools

Important:
- travel rules, entry requirements, pricing, and schedules can change quickly
- always verify critical travel details with official sources before travel
