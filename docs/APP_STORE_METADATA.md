## Description

Annai Travel Planner keeps your trip planning in one place.

Build trips, organize itinerary items, manage packing lists, track travel budgets, and keep important documents ready before you leave. When you want extra help, Annai Pro unlocks AI-assisted trip planning, destination guidance, local phrases, weather help, safety insights, and more.

Core features:
- create and manage trips
- build itinerary plans day by day
- track budgets and expenses
- store travel documents and confirmations
- manage packing lists
- get destination guidance and customs-entry support

Annai Pro includes:
- Ask Annai trip assistant
- AI trip plan generation
- AI safety and embassy guidance
- AI local phrases and weather support
- premium map and location tools

Important:
- travel requirements, pricing, hours, and entry rules can change quickly
- always verify critical travel details with official sources before departure
